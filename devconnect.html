About the event

The Fe team will be hosting an all day event on Sunday, April 24th at Tolhuistuin. Join us for discussions and presentations related to Fe, learn about Fe, help us shape Fe’s future, and geek out about smart contract language design and safety. Everyone is welcome!

The venue will have a projector for code sharing and presentations, whiteboards for mocking up ideas, and tables for hanging out. We’ll have materials prepared, but the event will not follow a strict format.

The space can accommodate up to 100 people at a time, but we expect that people will come and go throughout the day. If you are interested in attending, please fill out the form below to help give us a better idea of the audience. You might also receive email updates.

Click here to signup!

We are not planning on being selective, but if too many people want to attend, then we will select attendees based on their answers.




Schedule

Times: 10:00 - 17:00 (Doors @ 9:00, Hard deadline at 17:30)
Lunch: 12:00 - 13:00
